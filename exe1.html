Is avocado a fruit or a vegetable?

Let's start with a very important question for the Israeli community - is avocado a fruit or a vegetable? The answer is fruit. After answering this question, to the question for which we gathered: Is avocado fattening or not? Here are the nutritional facts. An avocado that weighs about 200 grams has over 300 calories, about 30 grams of fat, about 13 grams of dietary fiber and various other vitamins.

Recipe for Mexican guacamole:

Empty the inside of the avocado into a bowl and mash with a fork. Do not exaggerate the crushing, leave pieces of avocado whole and bite-sized. Add tomatoes, cilantro, onion and chili and lemon juice and mix gently with a spoon. Season with a little salt and black pepper.

The benefits of eating Avocado:

  1. healthy for the heart
  2. Dietary fiber - for the health of the digestive system
  3. Potassium - for the muscles
  4. Folic acid - lowers the risk of depression and disease
  5. Keeps the eyes
  6. It's very tasty :)

Despite the many health and nutritional benefits of avocados, it is important to remember that especially when it comes to old people, many of whom have a medical history, professionals should be consulted before making a dietary change or a diet that differs from the usual diet.
